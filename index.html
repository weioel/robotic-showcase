Knapp - automated pharmacy

About the System

The KNAPP application takes medication logistics to a new level. Our intelligent LARA 3 robot recognizes individual pharmaceutical packages, detects them visually and picks them up with a precise suction system. It then places them in a predefined location with millimeter precision - reliably, quickly and without errors. The automated pharmacy brings efficiency to order picking and takes the strain off pharmaceutical staff on a daily basis. Whether in hospital pharmacies or central distribution centers
